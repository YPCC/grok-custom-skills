Patient Journey Timeline (2026 Perspective)
1. Diagnosis & Workup
Colonoscopy, biopsy, MMR/MSI, PI3K testing (new), C/A/P CT, CEA
2. Biomarker Testing
Rapid MGPT: RAS, BRAF V600E, HER2, PI3K pathway, MMR/MSI
3. Surgery
Colectomy + en bloc LN. Liver/lung oligomet: Thermal ablation prioritized ≤3cm (COLLISION)
4. Adjuvant (by Biomarker)
pMMR: FOLFOX/CAPEOX ± aspirin (if PI3K+)
dMMR: FOLFOX + Atezolizumab (new)
5. Surveillance / Metastatic
CEA, CT, Colonoscopy. Metastatic: Biomarker-driven systemic therapy (Encorafenib, IO, HER2, KRAS G12C)
PI3K Pathway Details (New 2026)
Alterations included: PIK3CA exon 9/20, other PIK3CA, PIK3R1, PTEN mutations, deep PTEN deletions
Action: Aspirin 100–162 mg PO daily × 3 years post-surgery (can be concurrent with chemo)
Now mandatory molecular profiling for all stage II & III CRC
dMMR/MSI-H Key Updates
• Neoadjuvant IO for T4 or bulky primary (new)
• Adjuvant: FOLFOX/CAPEOX + Atezolizumab (new options)
• New dedicated page for unresectable metastases (COL-16)
• Expanded checkpoint inhibitors (incl. Penpulimab, more PD-L1 agents)
